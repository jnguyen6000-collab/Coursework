2. How might a cybercriminal use a tool like Wunderfind for malicious purposes?

   
	Cybercriminals might use Wunderfind to track any victim with a Bluetooth device. They will be able to pinpoint their location with ease. After they have figured out their location, they can start sending malware using Wunderfind as an attack vector. Wunderfund gives the names of all Bluetooth devices nearby. If they know the name, they can send malware by forcefully disconnecting them from their current Bluetooth device and tricking them into connecting to a fake one.
