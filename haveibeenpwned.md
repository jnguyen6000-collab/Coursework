# HaveIBeenPwned 

## Overview
Used HaveIBeenPwned to check whether a personal email address 
has appeared in any known data breaches. 

## Results
Email checked: [my school email]
Result: No data breaches found 

## What a Positive Result Would Mean
If breaches were found it would indicate that the email and potentially 
its associated password appeared in a leaked database. Best practice 
in that case would be to:
- Immediately change the password on the affected account
- Enable multi-factor authentication
- Monitor for suspicious account activity

## Why This Matters
Even a clean result is valuable — it confirms account is safe
